Admissions

Welcome to Distinct Children Academy!

We are excited to have you join our community. Our admissions process is simple and straightforward. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, etc.).
  3. Attend an interview with our admissions team.
  4. Receive your admission confirmation.

For any questions regarding the admissions process, please contact us.
